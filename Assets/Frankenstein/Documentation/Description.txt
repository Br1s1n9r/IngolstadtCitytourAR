PROFESSIONAL 3D MODEL FOR ANIMATIONS AND GAME.<br>
<br>
High quality and low poly model of Frankenstein.Frankenstein is perfect for your game, cartoon or any other project.<br>
<br>
Originally modelled in Maya.This Frankenstein is Rigged. <br>
<br>
This 3d model was made with care and detail in every particular, based on real-world objects. <br>
<br>
GEOMETRY- <br>
The model is made in MAYA. <br>
<br>
All model is 100% smoothable and easily editable. <br>
<br>
The level of detail of this 3d model is implemented for the most extreme close-up renderings. <br>
<br>
You can applied smooth to each object ( also smaller ones , bolts cables etc...) and you can have higher zoom, without losing detail. <br>
<br>
In FBX version maya 2015, <br>
<br>
Objects are textured by diffuse maps. <br>
<br>
No 3rd party plugin needed. <br>
